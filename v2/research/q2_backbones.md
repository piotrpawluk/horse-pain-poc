# Q2 — Frozen Video-Encoder Backbones for RHpE PoC (n≤1000/behavior)

## SOTA summary
On frozen-feature evaluation for fine-grained motion, the published evidence is unusually clean: **V-JEPA-2 ViT-g (1B) materially beats every alternative on the only benchmark resembling the RHpE regime — SSv2 attentive-probe**. V-JEPA-2 paper (arXiv:2506.09985, Table 4): V-JEPA-2 ViT-g 75.3 / ViT-g/384 77.3, **InternVideo2-1B 69.7, VideoMAE-V2 (1B) 56.1**, Diving-48 V-JEPA-2 90.2 vs DINOv2 82.5 — pixel-reconstruction (VideoMAE-V2) and image-only DINOv2 lose to feature-prediction under a frozen encoder. **Hiera, MViT-v2, Video-Swin do not appear in V-JEPA-2's frozen comparison**; their published wins (Hiera-L K400 +2.1pt, MViT-v2 SSv2 70.5, Video-Swin K400 84.9) are all **end-to-end fine-tuned, not frozen-probe** — that gap does not extrapolate to N≤500. **No animal-specific video foundation model exists** as of 2026-05; the closest signal is a TimeSformer fine-tuned on cattle (85% / 7-class, npj Vet Sci 2026), which is supervised FT, not a backbone.

## Per-backbone (verified checkpoints)
- **V-JEPA-2 ViT-g** — [github.com/facebookresearch/vjepa2](https://github.com/facebookresearch/vjepa2), 1B; **only configuration with documented frozen-probe gain over the ViT-L floor**.
- **VideoMAE-V2** ([CVPR 2023, arXiv:2303.16727](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.16727)) — Base/Large/Huge/Giant on [HF collection](https://huggingface.co/collections/OpenGVLab/videomae-v2); giant verified at [OpenGVLab/VideoMAEv2-giant](https://huggingface.co/OpenGVLab/VideoMAEv2-giant) (1B, 16×224). **Frozen-probe loss: -19 to -21 pts vs V-JEPA-2 at equal scale** — drop.
- **Hiera** ([ICML 2023 oral, arXiv:2306.00989](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.00989)) — Base/Base+/Large/Huge K400 ckpts on HF (e.g. [facebook/hiera_huge_16x224.mae_k400_ft_k400](https://huggingface.co/facebook/hiera_huge_16x224.mae_k400_ft_k400)); **no frozen low-N evidence published** — lateral, unverified.
- **MViT-v2** ([CVPR 2022, arXiv:2112.01526](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.01526)) — image via [timm/mvitv2_large_cls.fb_inw21k](https://huggingface.co/timm/mvitv2_large_cls.fb_inw21k); video via torchvision `mvit_v2_s` (K400-V1). **Fine-tuned-only evidence** — likely lateral.
- **Video Swin** ([CVPR 2022, arXiv:2106.13230](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.13230)) — only **community ckpts** ([Tonic/video-swin-transformer](https://huggingface.co/Tonic/video-swin-transformer), not official Microsoft); coarse-action benchmark only — **flagged, do not extrapolate to RHpE**.
- **InternVideo2** ([arXiv:2403.15377](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.15377), Shanghai AI Lab preprint) — [OpenGVLab/InternVideo2-Stage2_1B-224p-f4](https://huggingface.co/OpenGVLab/InternVideo2-Stage2_1B-224p-f4); SSv2 frozen 69.7 → **lateral-to-worse vs V-JEPA-2**.
- **Animal-specific** — [PFERD npj Sci Data 2024](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-024-03312-1) (equine pose, not video FM); cattle TimeSformer ([npj Vet Sci 2026](https://www.nature.com/articles/s44433-026-00004-x), supervised). **No frozen video FM available.**

## Cost-benefit on M2 Max (96 GB)
1B-param video encoders fit comfortably at fp16 (<6 GB weights, ~2-4 GB acts at bs=1, 16×224); 96 GB is non-binding. **PyTorch MPS works** for all listed backbones via standard `transformers`/`timm`/`torchvision`; **no MLX-native ports as of 2026-05**. Throughput (frozen extraction, MPS, bs=1, 16×224): ViT-L scale ~6-15 clips/s; 1B scale ~1-3 clips/s. A 15-min RHpE session ≈ 450 clips → 5 min wall on ViT-L, 15-25 min at 1B (offline-acceptable, not real-time). Feature dims (V-JEPA-2 ViT-L ~1024, ViT-g ~1408, VideoMAE-V2-g ~1408, Hiera-L 1152, MViT-v2-L 1152, Video-Swin-L 1536, InternVideo2-1B 1408) are **all comparable** — no dimensionality advantage that meaningfully shifts linear-probe sample efficiency at N≤500.

## What this implies for the PoC
**The single A/B worth running is V-JEPA-2 ViT-g vs the current V-JEPA-2 ViT-L floor** — the only configuration with published frozen-probe evidence of beating ViT-L on a fine-grained motion benchmark. Optional second-opinion candidate: **InternVideo2-Stage2-1B**, expecting lateral-to-worse. **Drop VideoMAE-V2, Hiera, MViT-v2, Video-Swin from the backbone shortlist** — none have published frozen low-N evidence on fine-grained behavior, and the one direct frozen comparison (VideoMAE-V2 1B at SSv2 56.1) is a 19-21 pt loss to V-JEPA-2 at equal scale.

### Open Questions
1. **Backbone choice likely is not the dominant bottleneck at N=100-500.** V-JEPA-2 ViT-L is already AUC 0.87-0.90 on ears; a +5-7 pt SSv2 frozen-probe gap from scaling to ViT-g is roughly the *upper bound* of what backbone selection buys at this N. **Q3 architectural decisions (per-behavior heads vs multi-task vs attentive probe vs temporal aggregation) plausibly dominate.** Lock the ViT-L floor; run a single ViT-g A/B *after* Q3 is settled.
2. **Hiera/MViT-v2/Video-Swin have zero published frozen-probe evidence at SSv2-class fine-grained.** If reconsidered, a Stage-1.5 single-behavior smoke test (frozen features, linear probe, source-LOSO) is mandatory before commitment.
3. **No animal-specific video foundation model exists.** Re-survey in 6 months; an equine/livestock-pretrained DINOv2/VideoMAE class model would warrant full re-evaluation.
