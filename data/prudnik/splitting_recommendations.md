## Method

Uniform splitting targeting **~10.0s windows** (midpoint of RME 5–15s range), with the constraint that all proposed windows fall in [5.0, 15.0]s. For each long clip with duration *D*:

1. `n = max(2, round(D / 10.0))`
2. `window_dur = D / n`
3. Adjust `n` until `window_dur ∈ [5.0, 15.0]`
4. Propose uniform splits at `i × window_dur` for `i = 0..n`

**This is mechanical, not content-aware.** Without watching each clip, optimal natural breakpoints can't be identified. Recommended workflow: use these timestamps as defaults, adjust during labeling if a window catches a transition awkwardly. Per the locked protocol (§5), borderline cases get a `?` suffix on the label.
